Research on the Cosmos

Inspired by the movie Interstellar, I have been exploring these topics for two years.

General Theory of Relativity

The theory formulated by Einstein, explaining gravity as the curvature of spacetime caused by mass and energy.

Black Holes

Regions of spacetime exhibiting gravitational acceleration so strong that nothing can escape from it, not even light.

Galaxies

Massive systems of stars, gas, dust, and dark matter, bound together by gravity. Home to countless mysteries!

Time

Not just a clock ticking — a dimension intertwined with space, affected by gravity and speed.

Gravity

The fundamental force that governs the motion of planets, stars, and galaxies. It's more than just a pull — it's the fabric of the universe.
